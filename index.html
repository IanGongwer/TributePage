Alan Turing

The man behind the machine

Alan Turing in his prime years of computing. This is the man that cracked the Enigma Code in July 1942.

Timeline of Alan Turing's Life

  • 1912 - Alan Mathison Turing was born
  • 1938 - Obtained PhD from Princeton University
  • 1940 - First Turing Bombe installed at Bletchley Park
  • 1941 - Proposed marriage to Joan Clarke
  • 1942 - Alan Turing and his colleagues break the German Naval Enigma system
  • 1943 - Turing works for top level intelligence link with USA regarding cryptology. Also worked at Bell laboratories on speech encypherment
  • 1945 - Joined National Physical Laboratory (NPL) to work on the Automatic Computing Engine (ACE)
  • 1946 - Presented course of lectures on Versions V, VI, and VII of the ACE design
  • 1953 - Published classic paper on computer chess
  • 1954 - Alan Turing dies of cyanide poisoning on June 7th. He was cremated at Woking crematorium
